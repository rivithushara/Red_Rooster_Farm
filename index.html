History

In the heart of Mathara District, where emerald paddy fields meet the sapphire embrace of the Indian Ocean, lies Red Rooster Farm. Its story, like the rooster's crow at dawn, begins in 1960, when Mr K. M. Perera , a young man with fire in his heart and grit in his hands, carved a patch of sun-soaked earth from the wilderness.

Driven by a dream bigger than the swaying coconut palms, owner envisioned a farm that sang with life, a symphony of vibrant crops and flourishing livestock. He named it "Red Rooster," a symbol of courage and tenacity, mirroring his own spirit as he tackled the challenges of starting anew.

The early years were a dance with dust and sweat. Calloused hands coaxed life from the soil, nurturing fields of golden rice and fragrant spices. Also in Livestock sector. Days were long, sun painting the sky in fierce hues, but hope bloomed alongside the crops. Every harvest was a victory, a testament to the unwavering spirit of the Red Rooster.

As whispers of the farm's bounty spread, like wind rustling through the palm groves, Red Rooster became a beacon of nourishment for the surrounding villages. Laughter and the clinking of plates filled the air as families gathered around steaming pots of rice, their hearts warmed by the generosity of the farm..

Today, Red Rooster stands tall, a symbol of agricultural harmony. Its fields, abuzz with life, offer not just sustenance but also a living testament to the unwavering spirit of its roots. The crow of the rooster still rings out, a chorus of resilience echoing through the generations, a call to nurture the land and nourish the community.

From Seed to Supper

Our story begins with the rich soil of our own farmland. We nurture a diverse range of vegetables, from crisp lettuces and juicy tomatoes to vibrant eggplants and fragrant herbs. Every step, from planting to harvest, is done with care and respect for the land.

Beyond the Greens

Our farm extends beyond the vegetable patch. We're proud to be home to a thriving coconut grove and a happy flock of chickens. Our coconuts are bursting with flavor, while our free-range chickens lay eggs with yolks as golden as the Matara sun.

More Than Just Produce:

At Red Rooster, we're more than just farmers - we're your neighbors. We're committed to supporting our community and building a sustainable future for Matara District. So come join us, not just for the freshest produce, but also for a taste of the Red Rooster spirit!
